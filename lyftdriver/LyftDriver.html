Lyft Driver App

2016 - 2019

Research Product Design Prototyping

Together with an incredible team of designers, researchers, and producers, I designed a suite of features to help Lyft drivers makes the most of their time on the road

Driving Smarter

Lyft drivers are working towards unique life goals - bootstrapping a business, paying for school, or buying a home. We designed tools to help drivers better understand when and where to drive in order to make the most of their time on the road, and hit their goals sooner.

Increasing driver activations

Getting started with Lyft can be a challenge for drivers who are unsure of what to expect. I designed a framework for bite-sized educationl lessons for newly approved Lyft drivers to put them at ease before their first ride.
